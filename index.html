The Kodkod


The kodkod is the smallest wildcat found in the Americas, but don't let its size fool you. The kodkod is a ferocious hunter which stalks and kills various small lizards, birds and rodents. It also enjoys snacking on moths and other insects.


The kodkod lives in the temperate rainforests of Chile and Argentina, and its habitat is threatened by persistent logging in that region. Please donate today to protect this adorable - yet fierce - creature!
